WELCOME!

Stanford Infant School is a three form entry school from Reception to Year Two, where we deliver a rich and creative curriculum to inspire and excite every child and celebrate diversity. Consequently, children come to school happy and eager to learn.

We work in true partnership with our parents and governors and are a community of learners. We are extremely proud of our school and hope that this website gives you a flavour of life here, but remember there can be no substitute for a personal visit; we therefore look forward to showing you round our school and answering any questions you have.
