Welcome to Blackwell Primary School

Blackwell Primary School is a vibrant school and we do our very best to ensure that your child flourishes throughout their time with us; developing a passion for learning, thriving as individuals and achieving their academic potential.

The children are taught to become caring and contributing members of the school society where values such as sharing, consideration, respect, politeness, hard work, determination and good behaviour are openly promoted.  We expect high standards of behaviour from the children and adults in our school.

We would love to be able to show you around our school, so please do call the school office, if you are interested in a place here.

Ms P. Crocker

Headteacher
