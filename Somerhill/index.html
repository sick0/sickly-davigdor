Welcome to our website!

Somerhill is a diverse and creative community where the individual is cherished, talents are nurtured and excellence is an everyday occurrence.

The school is situated in landscaped surroundings with a variety of trees and borders.  There is a hard-surface playground with floodlights as well as a full-sized sports field for team games and athletics.  There is also an adventure playground, a designated wildlife area and a variety of outdoor seating options.
